A BIT OF OUR HISTORY

The school was started in 1998 in the Doornpoort area. At first, they rented a part of another preschool as their only classroom. In 1999 the principal and owner Louise Limper bought a piece of ground also situated in the Doornpoort area and started to build her own school. In 2000 Canny kids’ academy was officially opened with a brand-new building and 4 classrooms

ABOUT OUR OWNER

Louise Limper is the Owner and Principal of canny kids’ academy. When she married, her and her husband moved to a close by, then small community called Doornpoort. Doornpoort is in the northern parts of Pretoria (not Pretoria North). She realized there were no English preschools in the area and seized the opportunity. Doornpoort was growing quickly and a lot of English-speaking families moved into the area. Louise saw that these families had no where to go so she started a small school. The school was so successful that in only two years they had the resources to move to a bigger location. Louise has been the principal and owner for 22 years.
