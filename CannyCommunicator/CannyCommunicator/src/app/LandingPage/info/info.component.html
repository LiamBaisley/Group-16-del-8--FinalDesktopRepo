ABOUT OUR CLASSES


TEDDY BEAR CLASS

For Ages 2-3. Includes development of language,communication and gross motor skills. Recognition of primary colour, numbers and basic shapes


LION CLASS

For Ages 3-4. Includes exposure to a greater variety of experiences with more emphasis on development on small motor skills like using a scissor and holding a cryon and pencil

DOLPHIN CLASS

For Ages 4-5. Includes development of a number of concepts through counting objects in a meaningfull way and the development of writing skills by focusing on a small motor development activities


GIRAFFE CLASS

For Ages 5-6. Includes building on knowlege gaind in previous classes with emphasis on reasoning, problem solving, mathmatical and nurting skills
